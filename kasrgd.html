KASARAGOD

It is the northernmost district of Kerala and was established in 1966

Kasaragod is a municipal town and administrative headquarters of Kasaragod district in the state of Kerala, India. Established in 1966, Kasaragod was the first municipal town in the district. It is the northernmost district of Kerala and is also known as Sapta Bhasha Sangama Bhumi.Kasaragod, the northernmost district of Kerala, was formed on May 24, 1984. Prior to the reorganisation of the State, Kasaragod was part of the South Kanara district. Kasaragod became a part of Malabar district following the reorganisation of State and became part of the Kerala State.

The district has famous tourist centres, including the international fame BEKAL FORT & BEACH. There are also places which can be converted to attract the tourists. Kasaragod is known as the Land of Gods, Forts, Rivers, Beautiful hills and lengthy sandy beaches.
